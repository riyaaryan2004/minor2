hourly_data.csv

Purpose:
Micro-level pattern analysis, HR alerts, future time-series modeling.

Feature	        Type	            Kya Represent Karta Hai	            ML / System Contribution
date	        Date	            Kaunsa din	                        Trend tracking
hour	        Integer (0–23)	    Din ka kaunsa hour	                Time-of-day behavior pattern
day_of_week	    Integer (0–6)	    Weekday ya weekend	                Mood/productivity pattern difference
avg_hr	        Float	            Us hour ka average heart rate	    Stress / activity indicator
max_hr	        Integer	            Us hour ka highest HR	            Sudden spike detection
min_hr	        Integer	            Us hour ka lowest HR	            Recovery depth
hr_std	        Float	            HR variability in that hour	        Instability / stress fluctuation
steps	        Integer	            Us hour total steps	                Physical activity intensity
hr_relative	    Float (optional)	avg_hr - resting_hr	                Stress above baseline




daily_data.csv

Purpose:
Mood prediction + Productivity estimation + Movie recommendation.

Feature	            Type	        Kya Represent Karta Hai	        ML / System Contribution
date	            Date	        Kaunsa din	                    Primary key
day_of_week	        Integer	        Weekday/weekend	                Behavioral variation
resting_hr	        Integer	        Baseline HR	                    Fitness + recovery level
total_steps	        Integer	        Pura din movement	            Activity score
total_sleep	        Integer(min)	Total sleep duration	        Mood & recovery major factor
deep_ratio	        Float	        Deep sleep %	                Physical recovery quality
rem_ratio	        Float	        REM sleep %	                    Emotional stability
sleep_deficit	    Integer	        Ideal sleep se kitna kam	    Mood risk indicator
sleep_start_time	Time (optional)	Kab soye	                    Sleep rhythm analysis
wake_time	        Time (optional)	Kab uthe	                    Routine stability
avg_hr_day	        Float	        Day ka average HR	            Overall stress level
hr_std_day	        Float	        HR variability entire day	    Mental fluctuation
activity_load	    Float	        Weighted exertion score	        Fatigue indicator
